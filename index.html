Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application ("NemanjaAI").

1. Information We Collect

  • Authentication Data: We use Firebase Authentication (including Google Sign-In) to identify users. This includes name, email, and profile photo.
  • Subscription Information: Payment is handled through Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not store credit card details.
  • Text Input Data: NemanjaAI processes the text you enter in order to generate AI responses. We do not store your text inputs permanently.
  • Usage Data: We collect basic usage info to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate and manage user accounts
  • Provide and improve AI chatbot features
  • Process subscriptions
  • Generate and return AI‑powered text responses

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with trusted third-party services such as Firebase for authentication, and Google Play or Apple App Store for subscription management. Please refer to Firebase's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy for more details. We use OpenAI’s API to generate chatbot responses. If you enable our AI-powered features, text inputs and outputs may be shared with OpenAI to provide and improve their services. OpenAI may temporarily store this data for safety and research purposes. You can learn more by reviewing OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

4. Security

We store your text conversation data for up to 7 days from your last activity. This temporary storage allows us to support chat history and ensure service reliability. Text may be sent to OpenAI during your session for generating responses. All text data is automatically deleted from our systems after 7 days of inactivity.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion or updates of your data by emailing us at ainemanjaapp@gmail.com.

6. Children

This app is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the app or on this page.

8. Contact

Email: ainemanjaapp@gmail.com

Политика приватности

Датум ступања на снагу: Decembar 1. 2025

Ова политика приватности објашњава како прикупљамо, користимо и делимо ваше личне податке када користите нашу мобилну апликацију ("NemanjaAI").

1. Подaци које прикупљамо

  • Подаци за аутентификацију: Користимо Firebase аутентификацију (укључујући Google Sign-In). Ово укључује име, е‑пошту и слику профила.

  • Подаци о употреби: Прикупљамо основне податке ради побољшања апликације.

3. Делитељност података

Не продајемо ваше личне податке. Можемо делити ваше податке са поверљивим трећим странама као што су Firebase за аутентификацију, као и Google Play или Apple App Store за обраду претплата.
